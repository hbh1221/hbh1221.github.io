My Favorite Recipe: Bacon Wrapped Dates

Final Result

Ingredients

  • Bacon

  • Dates

  • Toothpicks

Instructions

  • Cut bacon strips in half

  • Wrap around dates

  • Stick with toothpick

  • Put on tin-foiled pan

  • Bake at 350 degrees until bacon is cooked! Flip.
